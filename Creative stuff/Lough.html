Lough

People too angry,

Weather too cold,

Always yearning to be somewhere else,

I got my wish,

Found a better life, made a living,

Hard work and stress in a foreign land,

Abuse of alcohol, long hours and no friends,

Wash, rinse and repeat,

Would someone notice that I had gone missing,

Stepped over my end of road,

I was drunk,

A step away from depths of a lough.
